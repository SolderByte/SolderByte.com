About
Founded in the California in 2016, SolderByte's goal is to provide high quality affordable products.
SolderByte is built using open source technologies, and without open source technologies SolderByte would not have been able to accomplish its goal of making affordable products. It is commited in releasing its products and tools into the open source community, with the hopes that it will help others in the future.
